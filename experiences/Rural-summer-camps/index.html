Rural Summer Camps

Volunteer Experience with Enjoy Volunteering

Through my involvement with the non-profit organization Enjoy Volunteering, founded in 2012, I had the opportunity to participate in several rural summer and winter camps. My responsibilities included preparing educational games and reading picture books with children. These experiences allowed me to connect with students in underprivileged areas and make a meaningful contribution to their educational development.
